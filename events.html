Bridging Research, Policy and Practice: Final Conference

Our final conference, Bridging Research, Policy and Practice, will be held on 11th-12th Marc 2016, at the Free University of Brussels. The final programme can now be downloaded here: OSE Brussels Conference Programme and delegates can register at www.eventbrite.co.uk/e/cost-conference-2016-bridging-research-policy-and-practice-tickets-18306871345

We’re pleased to confirm that our conference will also include the launch of an exhibition of photographs taken by people with experience of supervision and by practitioners. The launch will also include a performance of songs inspired by these images and co-written by musicians, artists, academics and people with experience of supervision. The songs will be performed by Louis Abbott of the renowned Scottish indie band Admiral Fallow.

Conference Fees and Registration, drinks and food included

  • Early bird rate, register by 15th February 2016: €150
  • Standard rate (after 15th February 2016): €175
  • Concession rate available for the first 20 PhD students: €50 (first come first served basis)

Closing date for applications is 28th February 2016 (Early booking advised as places as limited)
For registration enquiries, please see the event registration page or contact Shona.Craven@glasgow.ac.uk
